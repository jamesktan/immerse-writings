بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
IN THE NAME OF GOD, THE MERCIFUL, THE COMPASSIONATE
1
القرآن:
وَاللَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
By the night enshrouding
2
القرآن:
وَالنَّهَارِ إِذَا تَجَلَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and the day in splendour
3
القرآن:
وَمَا خَلَقَ الذَّكَرَ وَالْأُنْثَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and That which created the male and the female,
4
القرآن:
إِنَّ سَعْيَكُمْ لَشَتَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
surely your striving is to diverse ends.
5
القرآن:
فَأَمَّا مَنْ أَعْطَىٰ وَاتَّقَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
As for him who gives and is godfearing
6
القرآن:
وَصَدَّقَ بِالْحُسْنَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and confirms the reward most fair,
7
القرآن:
فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُ لِلْيُسْرَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
We shall surely ease him to the Easing.
8
القرآن:
وَأَمَّا مَنْ بَخِلَ وَاسْتَغْنَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
But as for him who is a miser, and self-sufficient,
9
القرآن:
وَكَذَّبَ بِالْحُسْنَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and cries lies to the reward most fair,
10
القرآن:
فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُ لِلْعُسْرَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
We shall surely ease him to the Hardship;
11
القرآن:
وَمَا يُغْنِي عَنْهُ مَالُهُ إِذَا تَرَدَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
his wealth shall not avail him when he perishes.
12
القرآن:
إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا لَلْهُدَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
Surely upon Us rests the guidance,
13
القرآن:
وَإِنَّ لَنَا لَلْآخِرَةَ وَالْأُولَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and to Us belong the Last and the First.
14
القرآن:
فَأَنْذَرْتُكُمْ نَارًا تَلَظَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
Now I have warned you of a Fire that flames,
15
القرآن:
لَا يَصْلَاهَا إِلَّا الْأَشْقَى
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
whereat none but the most wretched shall be roasted,
16
القرآن:
الَّذِي كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
even he who cried lies, and turned away;
17
القرآن:
وَسَيُجَنَّبُهَا الْأَتْقَى
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and from which the most godfearing shall be removed,
18
القرآن:
الَّذِي يُؤْتِي مَالَهُ يَتَزَكَّىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
even he who gives his wealth. to purify himself
19
القرآن:
وَمَا لِأَحَدٍ عِنْدَهُ مِنْ نِعْمَةٍ تُجْزَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and confers no favour on any man for recompense,
20
القرآن:
إِلَّا ابْتِغَاءَ وَجْهِ رَبِّهِ الْأَعْلَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
only seeking the Face of his Lord the Most High;
21
القرآن:
وَلَسَوْفَ يَرْضَىٰ
ARTHUR JOHN ARBERRY:
and he shall surely be satisfied.
